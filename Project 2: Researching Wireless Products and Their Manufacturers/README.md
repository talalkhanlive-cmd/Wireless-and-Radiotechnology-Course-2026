# Project 2: Researching Wireless Products and Their Manufacturers

## Overview
This project focuses on researching wireless communication products and the companies that manufacture them. The purpose is to understand how wireless technologies are applied in real-world products.

## Objective
The objective of this project is to identify different wireless products, study their manufacturers, and analyze the wireless technologies used in these products.

## Methodology
The project is completed using the following steps:
1. Selection of commonly used wireless products.
2. Identification of manufacturers and product details.
3. Analysis of the wireless technology used (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, LTE, 5G, etc.).
4. Comparison of products based on features and applications.

## Tools and Sources
- Manufacturer official websites
- Product datasheets
- Online technical articles
- Course materials

## Outcome
This project improves understanding of practical wireless technologies and their implementation in commercial products.

## Conclusion
The research highlights the importance of wireless communication and shows how manufacturers integrate wireless technologies into modern devices.
