Major Wireless Products and Their Manufacturers

This repository presents an overview of important wireless communication products, highlighting their manufacturers, official reference material, and their main roles within modern wireless ecosystems.

1. LoRaWAN Gateway

Manufacturer: Kerlink
Reference: Kerlink Wirnet iStation – Official Product Page
Overview:
A LoRaWAN gateway functions as an intermediary between low-power IoT end devices and a centralized network server, enabling long-distance data communication. Kerlink gateways are designed for industrial use, offering strong reliability and high immunity to interference in large-scale outdoor deployments.

2. Zigbee Module

Manufacturer: Digi International
Reference: Digi XBee3 Zigbee 3.0 – Official Product Page
Overview:
Zigbee modules support low-power, short-range mesh networking, making them suitable for smart home systems and industrial sensing applications. The XBee3 family stands out due to its small size and the flexibility to operate across multiple wireless protocols using the same hardware platform.

3. Wi-Fi 6 Chipset

Manufacturer: Qualcomm
Reference: Qualcomm FastConnect 6900 – Official Product Page
Overview:
This chipset delivers high-performance wireless connectivity for mobile and consumer devices by supporting the Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) standard. It incorporates 4-stream Dual Band Simultaneous (DBS) technology, enabling multi-gigabit data rates and low-latency performance suitable for immersive applications such as virtual reality.

4. NB-IoT Module

Manufacturer: Quectel
Reference: Quectel BC660K-GL – Official Product Page
Overview:
NB-IoT modules provide cellular connectivity optimized for large-scale IoT deployments that demand deep indoor coverage and extended battery life. The BC660K-GL is a compact solution that supports multiple frequency bands while maintaining extremely low power consumption.

5. Sigfox Device

Manufacturer: UnaBiz (Sigfox)
Reference: Sens’it Discovery – Official Product Page
Overview:
Sigfox devices communicate over a global ultra-narrowband network, enabling long-range transmission of small data packets with minimal energy usage. The Sens’it device integrates multiple sensors, including temperature, humidity, and motion, making it suitable for environmental monitoring without the need for local device pairing.

6. Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) Beacon

Manufacturer: Kontakt.io
Reference: Kontakt.io Smart Beacon 2 – Official Product Page
Overview:
BLE beacons are compact transmitters used for indoor location services and asset tracking by broadcasting signals to nearby smart devices. The Smart Beacon 2 features a dual-battery system that supports up to four years of operation and simultaneously transmits both iBeacon and Eddystone signals.

7. Ultra-Wideband (UWB) Transceiver

Manufacturer: Qorvo
Reference: Qorvo DW3110 – Official Product Page
Overview:
UWB transceivers enable highly precise distance and location measurements, often achieving accuracy within a few centimeters. The DW3000 series is engineered for low-power operation and is well suited for automotive applications and mobile “find-my” tracking solutions.

8. Wi-Fi 6E Access Point

Manufacturer: Aruba (HPE)
Reference: Aruba 630 Series Campus AP – Official Product Page
Overview:
Wi-Fi 6E access points take advantage of the 6 GHz spectrum to reduce network congestion and improve throughput in enterprise environments. Aruba’s 630 series offers tri-band operation and advanced filtering to minimize interference across frequency bands.

9. 5G IoT Module

Manufacturer: Telit Cinterion
Reference: Telit FN990A Series – Official Product Page
Overview:
5G IoT modules deliver high-speed and low-latency connectivity for advanced applications such as industrial automation and high-resolution video monitoring. The FN990A series is an industrial-grade M.2 module supporting 3GPP Release 16, enabling global 5G Sub-6 GHz deployments.
